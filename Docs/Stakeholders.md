
# Stakeholders

# Enterprise Healthcare ETL Pipeline

---

## Purpose

This document identifies the key stakeholders involved in the Enterprise Healthcare ETL Pipeline project. It outlines the business decisions each stakeholder makes and the data required to support those decisions.

---

# 1. Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

## Role
Responsible for the overall strategic direction and performance of the hospital network.

### Key Decisions
- Is the hospital meeting its operational goals?
- Which hospitals or departments perform best?
- Where should investments be made?
- Which areas require immediate attention?

### Required Data
- Total patient admissions
- Revenue by hospital
- Patient satisfaction trends
- Department performance
- Hospital occupancy rates
- Executive KPI dashboard

---

# 2. Chief Operating Officer (COO)

## Role
Oversees daily hospital operations and resource management.

### Key Decisions
- Which departments require additional staff?
- Where are operational bottlenecks?
- How can patient wait times be reduced?
- How efficiently are hospital resources being used?

### Required Data
- Appointment volumes
- Average waiting time
- Department workload
- Doctor utilization
- Emergency room activity
- Bed occupancy

---

# 3. Hospital Manager

## Role
Manages day-to-day operations at an individual hospital.

### Key Decisions
- Is the hospital operating efficiently?
- Which departments need improvement?
- Are patient services meeting expectations?

### Required Data
- Daily patient visits
- Appointment status
- Doctor schedules
- Department performance
- Resource utilization

---

# 4. Finance Team

## Role
Responsible for financial planning, budgeting, and revenue analysis.

### Key Decisions
- Which departments generate the most revenue?
- Are billing processes efficient?
- How can operational costs be reduced?
- What are the hospital's monthly financial trends?

### Required Data
- Billing transactions
- Revenue by department
- Payment status
- Outstanding balances
- Insurance claims
- Monthly financial reports

---

# 5. Pharmacy Department

## Role
Manages medicine inventory and prescription fulfillment.

### Key Decisions
- Which medicines require replenishment?
- Which drugs are prescribed most frequently?
- Are inventory levels sufficient?
- Are any medicines nearing expiration?

### Required Data
- Prescription records
- Medicine inventory
- Stock availability
- Supplier information
- Daily medicine usage

---

# 6. Laboratory Manager

## Role
Oversees laboratory operations and diagnostic testing.

### Key Decisions
- Which tests are requested most frequently?
- Are laboratory reports delivered on time?
- Is laboratory workload balanced?
- Are testing resources sufficient?

### Required Data
- Laboratory test requests
- Test completion times
- Pending reports
- Technician workload
- Daily test volume

---

# 7. Doctors

## Role
Provide medical care and monitor patient treatment.

### Key Decisions
- Review patient history before consultation.
- Monitor treatment progress.
- Order laboratory tests.
- Prescribe medications.

### Required Data
- Patient medical history
- Appointment schedule
- Laboratory results
- Prescription history
- Diagnosis records

---

# 8. Data Analyst (Project Owner)

## Role
Designs the ETL pipeline, analyzes healthcare data, and delivers business insights through dashboards and reports.

### Key Decisions
- How should data be transformed and validated?
- Which KPIs should be monitored?
- How can reporting be automated?
- What insights can improve operational efficiency?

### Required Data
- Patient records
- Appointment data
- Billing information
- Pharmacy transactions
- Laboratory results
- Operational KPIs
- Historical trends

---

# Summary

| Stakeholder | Primary Focus |
|-------------|---------------|
| CEO | Business performance and strategic decisions |
| COO | Operational efficiency and resource management |
| Hospital Manager | Daily hospital operations |
| Finance Team | Revenue, billing, and financial reporting |
| Pharmacy Department | Medicine inventory and prescriptions |
| Laboratory Manager | Laboratory performance and testing efficiency |
| Doctors | Patient care and clinical decisions |
| Data Analyst | ETL, reporting, analytics, and dashboard development |
